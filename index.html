At Bike Helmets Save Lives we aim to see all bikers in the Boston area wearing helmets by 2025

  • "Children (5-14 years) and adolescents (15-19 years) have the highest rates of nonfatal bicycle-related injuries, accounting for more than one-third of all bicycle-related injuries seen in U.S. emergency departments." -CDC
  • "A Bike helmet reduces the risk for bicycle-related head injury by 74%-85%"-CDC
  • Community lead efforts are one of the most effective ways to increase helmet use.-CDC
